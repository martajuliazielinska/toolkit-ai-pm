## Project Overview

**Elevator Pitch:** A mobile application for managing a family's home budget, integrating bank accounts, savings goals, and financial education for children in one place.

**Product Vision:** To create Poland's leading digital platform for family finance management, which, thanks to personalization and educational tools, helps families build financial security and teach children good financial habits.

**Product Owner:** Dariusz Mozgowoj | **Version:** 2025-09-01

## Business Goals

**Q1 2026:** Launch MVP with integration of 3 largest banks and manual expense tracking module. Acquire 10,000 active families and establish partnership with one financial blog. Focused on the Polish market only.

**Q2 2026:** Introduce child pocket money module and achieve 1,000 paid subscriptions.

## Business Model

**Revenue Streams:**
- Freemium subscription (basic free, premium with advanced analytics)
- Commission fees for financial products (loans, insurance)
- Personalized financial plans
- Anonymous B2B data insights

**Operating Model:** SaaS platform with mobile app for families and web panel for financial advisors.

## Product Modules

- **Family Dashboard** - Account aggregation, manual expense entry, budget status, savings goals overview
- **Budgeting & Analytics** - Spending trends, expense categorization, budget alerts, financial reports
- **Child's Pocket Money** - Automated allowances, task-based rewards, savings goals, parental controls
- **Bank Integrations** - API synchronization with banks and financial institutions
- **Financial Education** - Interactive lessons, articles, family savings goal tracking
