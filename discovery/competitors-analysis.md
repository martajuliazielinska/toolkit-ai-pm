# Analiza Konkurentów - Akademicki Case

## Notion
- Features: Database, Wiki, Docs, Templates
- Pricing: $4-10/user/month
- Strengths: Uniwersalny, integracje
- Weaknesses: Slow performance, learning curve
- Market Position: Business/Enterprise

## Obsidian
- Features: Note-taking, Local-first, Vault system
- Pricing: $0 (free) + $96/year (sync)
- Strengths: Privacy, local storage, extensible
- Weaknesses: No collaboration, limited web presence
- Market Position: Individual/Creative professionals

## Monday.com
- Features: Project management, Automation, Reporting
- Pricing: $8-16/user/month
- Strengths: Visual, Easy collaboration
- Weaknesses: Expensive for large teams
- Market Position: Enterprise/Project teams
